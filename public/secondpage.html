Actors

Chadwick Boseman

Chadwick Aaron Boseman was an American actor, writer, and director who portrayed Black historical figures and the superhero Black Panther. He was known for his roles as Jackie Robinson in 42 and James Brown in Get on Up, and also starred as Thurgood Marshall in the 2017 film Marshall. Boseman's career spanned two decades and earned him an Academy Award nomination, a Golden Globe, and a Primetime Emmy Award.

Terry Crews

Terry Alan Crews is an American actor, television host, and former football player. He played Julius Rock in the UPN/CW sitcom Everybody Hates Chris, which aired from 2005 to 2009, and portrayed Terry Jeffords in the Fox and NBC sitcom Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

Chris Rock

Christopher Julius Rock is an American actor, comedian, and filmmaker who rose to fame in the 1980s for his stand-up routines. His observational comedy often covers race relations and human sexuality. Rock dropped out of high school early, but later earned his GED and studied the subject in community college before pursuing acting and comedy. In an interview with The Hollywood Reporter, Rock revealed that he was diagnosed with a learning disorder in his 50s.
